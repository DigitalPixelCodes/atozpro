Dubai Visa Types We Process

From tourist visits to long-term work permits — we handle it all.

01 — Tourist Visa

Dubai Tourist Visa

Planning a vacation to Dubai? We process 30-day and 90-day tourist visas with minimal documentation. Whether it's your first visit or a return trip, we make it seamless.

Processing time: 3–5 working days
Documents: Passport copy, photo, travel itinerary

02 — Business Visa

Dubai Business Visa

Attending a conference, meeting clients, or exploring business opportunities in Dubai? Our business visa service includes invitation letter coordination and fast-track processing.

Processing time: 3–5 working days
Documents: Passport, company letter, invitation (if any)

03 — Work Visa

Dubai Work / Employment Visa

Got a job offer in Dubai? We handle the complete work visa process — from labour card and medical test coordination to Emirates ID. We guide you and your employer through every step.

Processing time: 7–15 working days
Documents: Passport, offer letter, educational certificates, photos

04 — Family Visa

Dubai Family / Spouse Visa

Want to sponsor your spouse, children, or parents in Dubai? We handle salary certificate preparation, tenancy contract coordination, and the full documentation process.

Processing time: 10–20 working days
Documents: Sponsor's salary cert, marriage/birth cert, tenancy, passport copies

Transit & Student Visas

Transit Visa: 48 or 96-hour visas for layovers. Perfect for exploring Dubai during a stopover.

Student Visa: For students admitted to UAE universities. We coordinate with institutions and handle the full process including medical and Emirates ID.
